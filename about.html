About Us

RentUs is a company that provides rental services for utensils and cutlery for various occasions. We aim to make your events more convenient and hassle-free by delivering high-quality utensils to your doorstep. Whether you need utensils for a birthday party, a wedding, a corporate event, or any other occasion, we have you covered. You can choose from our wide range of packages that suit your budget and preferences. We also offer easy online booking, flexible payment options, and reliable customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I book a package?

    You can book a package online by filling out the form on our website. You will need to provide some details such as your name, email, phone number, address, date and time of the event, number of guests, and the package you want to book. Once you submit the form, we will confirm your booking and send you an invoice.

  • How do I pay for the package?

    You can pay for the package online using your credit card, debit card, or PayPal account. You can also pay cash on delivery when we deliver the utensils to you.

  • How do I cancel or modify my booking?

    You can cancel or modify your booking by contacting us at least 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time. You can call us at +91-1234567890 or email us at info@rentus.com. We will process your request and refund your payment if applicable.

  • How do I return the utensils?

    You can return the utensils by packing them in the same boxes that we delivered them in. We will pick up the utensils from your address at the agreed time. You do not need to wash or clean the utensils before returning them.

  • What if I damage or lose any of the utensils?

    We understand that accidents can happen sometimes. If you damage or lose any of the utensils, you will need to pay a replacement fee according to the price list that we provide with each package. The replacement fee will be deducted from your security deposit or added to your final bill.
